<h1>
  <span class="headline">What's Your Setup?</span>
  <span class="subhead">Instructor Guide</span>
</h1>

This is a discovery/discussion module, intended to get students investigating their own tech setups.

Try to open up the room for discussion as much as possible. You may want to lean on Slack to get more people sharing.

If people find unique or interesting things along the way as they investigate their setups you should suggest that they share photos of what they've found in Slack/Zoom (if the student feels comfortable with that) so others can see. This is especially useful if they're unsure of what they've found.
